// About

NUMBERS DON'T LIE. MARKETS DO.

I'm a Certified Public Accountant with over two decades of hands-on experience at the intersection of tax law and financial markets. My background gives me a lens most investors don't have — I think in after-tax returns, capital efficiency, and structural downside protection.

My investment philosophy is simple in principle and disciplined in practice: protect capital first, then hunt for asymmetric opportunities when the market's volatility creates them. Fear and dislocation are not problems to avoid — they are pricing events to exploit, with the right position sizing and risk framework.

Nothing here is financial advice. These are my personal views, shaped by two decades of reading markets, balance sheets, and the tax code.
